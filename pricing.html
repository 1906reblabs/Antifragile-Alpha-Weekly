§02

Top 10 Undervalued Stocks Free

Full screened table: company, ticker, exchange, sector, price, fair value, upside %, risk level, thesis summary, catalyst, and instrument access.

§03

Screening Methodology Free

How the 10 stocks were selected from 600+ candidates: factor scores, regime overlays, catalyst validation, and this edition's specific reasoning.

§04

High-Conviction Bets 🔒 Locked

3 stocks with full hidden truth thesis, exact entry range, base-case target, hard stop price, Risk:Reward ratio, bull/bear scenarios, and position sizing.

§05

One 5×–10× thesis per edition with explicit downside floor, sizing guidance (always ≤5%), catalyst window, and instrument. Currently: Southern Palladium (SPD).

§06

§07

Broker Matrix 🔒 Locked

Which SA brokers offer what instruments on JSE, A2X, and AltX. Fee comparison, availability, and best-fit broker per pick.

§08

Portfolio Construction 🔒 Locked

Regime-adjusted barbell allocations, explicit delta table vs prior edition (what changed and why), rand-hedge mandate check, and rebalancing rules.

§09

Risk Analysis & Scenarios 🔒 Locked

§10

Entry Guidelines 🔒 Locked

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get access after paying? ▾
After payment via PayFast, you'll receive an email with a link to the full unlocked report within a few minutes. For monthly and annual subscribers, new reports are emailed automatically every Friday. If you don't receive your email within 15 minutes, check your spam folder or contact us.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▾
Yes. Email us at research@antifragile-alpha.co.za at any time to cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you retain full access until then. We do not charge cancellation fees.
What payment methods are accepted? ▾
All payments are processed by PayFast, South Africa's leading payment gateway. We accept Visa, Mastercard, Instant EFT (all major SA banks), SnapScan QR, Capitec Pay, Masterpass, and Ozow EFT. We do not accept cash, crypto, or international cards that don't support ZAR billing.
Is the free preview really free forever? ▾
Yes. Sections 01–03 of every weekly edition are always free — no registration required. They're published on this site and on our public GitHub repository. We believe the free content demonstrates the quality; the locked sections deliver the actionable intelligence.
What is the Annual plan archive? ▾
Annual subscribers get access to all locked sections (§04–10) of every past edition, including the internal UNLOCKED copies with complete conviction theses, hard stop tables, and portfolio construction notes. This includes Edition 01 (Apr 1), Edition 02 (Apr 9), and every edition going forward.
Do you offer refunds? ▾
Single-issue purchases are non-refundable once the report has been accessed or delivered. For subscriptions, we offer refunds within 7 days of purchase in cases of technical failure preventing access. We do not offer refunds based on investment outcomes — the reports are informational, not advice. See our Terms of Use for full details.
How often are reports published? ▾
Weekly, every Friday. In volatile or regime-changing weeks (like the Apr 9–11 trade war cluster), we may publish additional interim editions at no extra cost to subscribers. These regime-change editions are a core part of the service — not an upsell.
Is this financial advice? ▾
No. Antifragile Alpha is not registered as a Financial Services Provider (FSP) with the FSCA. All content is for informational and educational purposes only. Before making any investment decision, you should consult a licensed financial advisor appropriate to your circumstances. Please read our full Financial Disclaimer.
What is the Institutional tier? ▾
The Institutional tier is for asset managers, family offices, hedge funds, and corporate prop desks. It includes everything in Annual, plus multi-seat access, custom sector research, direct analyst Q&A sessions, portfolio overlay consultations, and white-label report options. Pricing starts at R5,000/month and is negotiated based on team size and requirements. Contact us to discuss.
Which exchanges do you cover? ▾
We screen over 600 stocks across JSE Main Board, A2X Markets, AltX (JSE Growth Board), Cape Town Stock Exchange (CTSE), and 4 Africa Exchange (4AX). We intentionally cover under-researched small-caps on AltX and CTSE where analyst neglect creates the most mispricing opportunity.
