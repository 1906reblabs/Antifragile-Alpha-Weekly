Our Philosophy

Most SA equity research is either too generic — consensus broker notes recycling the same top-40 names — or too inaccessible, locked behind institutional pay-gates. Antifragile Alpha sits in the gap: institutional-grade research, priced for private investors and active traders.

We reject the idea that cheap P/E ratios are sufficient. Every pick must begin with what Peter Thiel called a secret — something that is true about a company that the majority of the market is not yet pricing. Without that, value stays cheap indefinitely.

"The antifragile investor doesn't panic at regime changes — they re-screen fast, rotate deliberately, and find the new asymmetries before the crowd catches up."
🔷

Thiel Framework — The Hidden Truth

Every pick must answer: what is true about this company that the market is not yet pricing correctly? Consensus ideas and crowded trades are disqualified by definition. The non-obvious insight is the starting point — never the finishing touch.

🔶

Taleb Framework — Antifragility

We prioritise asymmetric payoffs — limited downside with explosive upside — and positions that are convex: they benefit from volatility rather than being destroyed by it. Fragile balance sheets and linear return profiles are disqualifying criteria.

⚖️

Barbell Strategy — Structured Risk

50–60% stable defensive core. 30–40% high-upside mispriced opportunities. 5–10% moonshot ideas with 5×–10× potential. The structure is not static — it is regime-adjusted weekly. In a commodity bull, gold miners migrate into the core. In risk-off, we rotate defensively.

🚨

Regime Detection — Dynamic Overlay

Before any stock is screened, we run a macro regime check. If gold moves >20% in 4 weeks, the rand weakens >5%, the SARB revises its rate path, or an oil shock materialises — the barbell reweights immediately. The Apr 9 edition was the first live test. It worked.

The 4-Agent Intelligence Loop

Each weekly edition is produced by a structured 4-agent research loop. This is what separates Antifragile Alpha from a single analyst's view — it's a closed system that improves itself every edition through a mandatory feedback and learning cycle.

01
Scanner Agent
Opportunity Discovery
Screens 600+ JSE/A2X/CTSE/4AX stocks for unusual price movements, volume spikes, earnings catalysts, and sector dislocations. Outputs a raw opportunity list of 20–40 candidates. No deep analysis — pure signal detection.
02
Analyst Agent
Thesis Builder
Applies Thiel + Taleb + barbell frameworks to the raw list. Kills ideas without clear catalysts, fragile balance sheets, or consensus theses. Each surviving stock must have a "hidden truth" that articulates why the market is wrong. Outputs 10–15 refined picks.
03
Portfolio Agent
Capital Allocator
Selects top 10 picks. Assigns 3 high-conviction bets and 1 moonshot. Constructs the regime-adjusted barbell. Maps instruments (shares, SSFs, CFDs) and brokers. Sets hard stop prices for every position. Publishes the weekly report.
04
Auditor Agent
Learning Engine
Tracks past pick performance. Diagnoses what worked and what failed. Updates the SKILLS file with new rules. Maintains the running MEMORY_LOG. This is the feedback mechanism that makes the system antifragile — it learns from every edition, including the losses.

The Auditor Agent's output feeds directly back into the next Scanner and Analyst run. Stop-loss discipline, regime triggers, and catalyst window rules are all derived from edition-level post-mortems. The Apr 9 oil shock tightened the speculative catalyst window from ≤6m to ≤4m. The SOL stop-loss validated the discipline. These are live, tested rules.
